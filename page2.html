About Us
At Circuit Automations, we help people get back the hours they lose every week to boring, repetitive work. Instead of spending all day clicking, copying, or updating spreadsheets, our automations take care of it for you — quietly running in the background while you focus on the stuff that actually grows your business.

We build systems that connect the tools you already use, pass information between them, and keep everything up to date without you having to lift a finger. It might be something simple, like automatically organizing your files, or more complex, like creating a full chain of tasks that trigger the moment a customer signs up.

Every setup we make is tailored to the way you work. We don’t believe in forcing you into some “one-size-fits-all” template. Instead, we take the time to figure out what’s slowing you down, then design a solution that quietly handles it — so you can spend less time managing tasks and more time moving forward.
